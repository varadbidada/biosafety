## Q&A PREPARATION

### Expected Questions & Answers:

**Q: How accurate is it?**
A: 83% accuracy with our ensemble model. Better than 70% of existing systems.

**Q: What data do you use?**
A: Climate data (rainfall, temperature, NDVI), historical case counts, 17 features total.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Open-source technology. Deployment costs: ~₹50L/year for nationwide coverage.

**Q: Can it predict other diseases?**
A: Yes! Architecture is adaptable. We're exploring malaria and chikungunya next.

**Q: How long to deploy?**
A: 3-month pilot in select districts, then 6 months for nationwide rollout.

**Q: What about data privacy?**
A: Aggregated district-level data only. No personal information collected.
